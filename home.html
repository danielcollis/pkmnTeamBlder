Pokemon Team Builder
Pokedex: Type Chart: Showdown:

This webpage can aid in creating a balanced Pokemon team that can respond to any type in the game. After selecting your team from the menus below, you can view how your Pokemon match up defensively against attacks from any type in the game! Hopefully, after using this resource, your team will have a response to any situation.

This tool has been developed to be used exclusively with Gen 5 Pokemon, which is great, since Gen 5 is the best Pokemon generation ever. Support for other generations will be added in the future!
